Welcome to "THE BAND SIX offical website. The Band Six is a six piece band, lead by music director Gil Smith(II) Jr. Known for its performances with YG Entertainment artists such as Big Bang, 2NE1 and PSY as well as number of artists through out Asia. The band got there break during the BIGBANG's Alive Tour back in 2012, which took them to various places around the world, mainly in Asia and the United Kingdom. Currently BAND SIX is the live band for BIGBANG's Made Tour. For more inforamtion regarding Band SIX please vist the Biogrpaghy, Schedule, Videos, Gallary.
